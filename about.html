The thesis

Every client has a story. My job is to capture it.

Hi, I'm Dan.

I'm a photographer and graphic designer based in Wheaton, IL, running a small creative agency for professionals, creatives, and local businesses across the Chicago suburbs.

I launched my studio to help tell stories. I've always been fascinated by the power an image can convey. A portrait. An advertisement. Even a business card or website.

Every client has a story, and my job is to capture it and share it with the world. A new job. Chasing a dream. Launching or growing a business. Starting a family. It is an honor to be the one documenting that moment with you.

When I'm not shooting, I'm spending quality time with my family and writing our story. My camera is never too far away.
